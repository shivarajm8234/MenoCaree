Hormone Therapy

Learn about hormone therapy and its benefits for menopause management.

Benefits of Hormone Therapy:

  • Reduces hot flashes
  • Improves sleep
  • Prevents bone loss
  • Enhances mood

Introduction

Hormone therapy can be an effective treatment for managing menopause symptoms. Understanding the different types can help women make informed decisions.

Types of Hormone Therapy

Hormone therapy can include:

  • Estrogen therapy
  • Combined hormone therapy
  • Low-dose vaginal products

Risks and Benefits

While hormone therapy can alleviate symptoms, it may also have risks such as blood clots and breast cancer. Discuss these with your doctor.

FAQs

  • How long should I stay on hormone therapy? The duration varies based on individual needs and should be discussed with a healthcare provider.
  • Can hormone therapy help with mood swings? Yes, hormone therapy can help stabilize mood swings for some women.
